Features

Track your performance

Don't rely on just your sales data to track how your beers perform. With Brewlytics, you can see data from venues to measure things like ratings, comments, as well as see who's drinking your beer and what they usually drink.

See what drinkers are saying

Integrations with your social media accounts lets you overaly your in venue performance with live social media data. Improve the performance of your social media campaigns and optimise your online presence.

Simple and cheap pricing

No complicated or tiered plans. Simple pay per month pricing lets you control your subscription and you can cancel anytime. Your data is your own, so if you do decide to cancel, you can export your data and take it with you.
